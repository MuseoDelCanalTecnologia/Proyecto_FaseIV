CANAL EXPANSION PROYECT




The expansion project involved the construction of two new lock complexes and a third transit lane to allow for the passage of larger vessels with increased cargo capacity.

o this end, the project was divided into five key components, encompassing the design and construction of the new locks, dredging operations, and water supply enhancements. This project started on September 3, 2007, and the expanded canal was inaugurated on June 26, 2016.
