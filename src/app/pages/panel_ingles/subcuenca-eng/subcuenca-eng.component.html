SUBWATERSHEDS AND MAIN RIVERS






The Canal watershed contains great number of rivers and subwatersheds.

In these regions, thePanama Canal Authority has worked in collaboration with the communities that inhabit them to protect and conserve the environment through socio-environmental agroforestry programs, a production system that uses the land in a more sustainably by combining the cultivation of timber trees with agricultural or livestock farming.
