GATUN LAKE RECREATIONAL NATURAL AREA






Creation date: 1985
Area: 3.47km2
Location: Colon province
Information: this protected area is a tourist attraction due to various activities that can be carried out, such as fishing and hiking. It also features a variety of flora and fauna including the white-nosed coati (Nasua narica), the Rothschild's porcupine (Nasua narica), el puerco espín (Coendou rothschildi) y el ñeque (Dasyprocta punctata).
