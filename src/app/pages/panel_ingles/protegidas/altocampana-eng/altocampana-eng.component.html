ALTOS DE CAMPANA NATIONAL PARK AND BIOLOGICAL RESERVE






Creation date: : 1966
Area: 49 km2
Location: Panama Oeste province
Information:This protected area provides water to more than 6,000 people. It also is home to over 540 species of plants, 26 of them being endemic, as well as 39 mammal species, 267 bird species, 86 reptile species, and 62 amphibian species, the golden frog being one of them.
