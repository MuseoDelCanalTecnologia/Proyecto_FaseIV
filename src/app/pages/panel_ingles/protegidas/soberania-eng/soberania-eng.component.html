SOBERANIA NATIONAL PARK






Creation date: 1980
Area: 1,950 km2
Location: Panama and Colon provinces
Information: Soberanía National Park features 36 km2 of trails where you can find more than 800 species of animals, with a combination of Central and South American species such as the capybara (Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris). (Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris).
