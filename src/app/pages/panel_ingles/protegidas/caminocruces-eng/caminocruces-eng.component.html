CAMINO DE CRUCES NATIONAL PARK






Creation date: 1992
Area: 40 km2
Location: Panama province
Information: : in addition to its historical value for containing part of the Cruces trail, which gives it its name, this park is characterized by its tropical humid forestsu where various types of trees such as ceibas (Ceiba pentandra), nance (Byrsonima crassifolia), and guayacan (Tabebuia guayacan) can be found.
