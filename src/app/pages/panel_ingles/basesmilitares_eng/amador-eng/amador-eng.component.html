AMADOR






Inauguration date: 1912
Original name: Fort Amador
Previous use: constructed together with Fort Grant with the objective of defending the entry to the Canal from the Pacific, using two artillery batteries. In 1914, besides the Army, the naval sector that housed the Radial Station of Balboa was established.

Reversion dates: 1979, 1996
Current name: Amador
Current use: although certain areas of the former fort were abandoned and other buildings were demolished, this sector, together with former Fort Grant, was given a touristic focus with convention centers, hotels, museums, restaurants and bars
