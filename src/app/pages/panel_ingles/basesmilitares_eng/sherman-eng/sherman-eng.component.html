SHERMAN






Inauguration date: : 1914
Original name: Fort Sherman
Previous use: constructed to defend the western side of the Canal’s Atlantic entry with seven artillery batteries. Since the 1950s, it served as the headquarters of the Southern Command’s Jungle Operations Training Center.

Reversion date: : 1999
Current name: Sherman
Current use: partially in abandonment. In Limon Bay there is a hotel and a marina for touristic purposes.
