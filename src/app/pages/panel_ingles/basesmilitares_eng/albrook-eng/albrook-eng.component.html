ALBROOK






Inauguration date: 1924
Original name: Albrook Air Force Station
Previous use: the first facility in the Pacific sector to be used as a military airport, serving as a base for the American Air Force until its reversion.

Reversion date: 1997
Current name: Albrook
Current use: part of the air force base was transformed into the Marcos A. Gelabert international airport. Other uses given to this military base were the construction of the Great Transport Terminal and the Albrook Mall shopping Centre.
