DE LESSEPS






Inauguration date: 1911
Original name: Fort De Lesseps
Previous use: constructed to defend the eastern side of the Canal’s Atlantic entry with an artillery battery called Morgan.

Reversion date: 1955
Current name: : Fort De Lesseps and Morgan Artillery Battery
Current use: Only Morgan Artillery Battery remains in place, and it functions as regional headquarter of Panama’s Tourism Authority and as a touristic spot.
