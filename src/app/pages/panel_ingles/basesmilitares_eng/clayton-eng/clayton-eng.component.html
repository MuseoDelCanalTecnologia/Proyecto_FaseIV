CLAYTON






Inauguration date: 1922
Original name: Fort Clayton
Previous use: housed the base of the Southern Command of the Army of the United States of America.

Reversion date: 1999
Current name: Ciudad del Saber (“City of Knowledge”)
Current use: what previously was Fort Clayton is currently the location of the international knowledge production and exchange center called Ciudad del Saber, that houses different educational centers, aside from having offices of several non-governmental organizations (NGO).
