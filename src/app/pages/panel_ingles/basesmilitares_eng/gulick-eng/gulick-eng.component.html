GULICK






Establishment date: 1941
Original name: Fort Gulick
Previous use: from 1949 until its reversion, Fort Gulick was the base of operations of the School of the Americas

Reversion dates: 1984 and 1999
Current name: Fort Espinar
Current use: residential use. A hotel was established in the buildings belonging to the former School of the Americas.
