RODMAN






Construction date: 1937
Original name: Rodman Naval Station
Previous use: important base during World War II, since it was the main center of operations on the Pacific side of Central and South America, supporting the United States and allied military ships.

Reversion date: 1999
Current name: Rodman Port/PSA International Terminal
Current use: location of the National Aeronaval Service of Panama. In 2010, a port terminal managed by the PSA Panama International Terminal started operations.
